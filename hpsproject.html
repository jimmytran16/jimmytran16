HPS Portal

Project Description

Created a website that would help After School Programs and Public Schools better communicate their data and understand their students more. This website includes a user log in page, activity log's page to log user's activity in real time, Student behavior page to keep track of their progress by using graphs, submission forms page to upload any disciplanary/incident that happened throughout the day.
